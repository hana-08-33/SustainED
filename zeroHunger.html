The Challenge of Hunger

Hunger affects millions of people worldwide, and despite progress in some areas, global food insecurity remains a significant issue. Many people are deprived of their basic nutritional needs, which leads to malnutrition, disease, and early death. Addressing the root causes of hunger is vital for a sustainable and healthy future for all.
