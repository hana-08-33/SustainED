The Challenge of Gender Inequality

Gender inequality is a widespread issue affecting many aspects of society, from the workplace to education. Women and girls are often marginalized, deprived of opportunities, and subject to violence and discrimination. To ensure a better future for all, gender equality must be achieved globally.

Key Statistics

  • Global gender gap: Women earn 23% less than men globally.
  • Violence against women: 1 in 3 women will experience physical or sexual violence in their lifetime.
  • Education disparity: Over 130 million girls are out of school worldwide.
