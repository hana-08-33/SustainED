The Challenge of Poverty

Poverty is a complex and multifaceted issue that affects millions of people around the world. It deprives individuals of their basic human rights and prevents them from reaching their full potential. Extreme poverty persists in many regions, while others struggle with vulnerability and inequality. We must address the root causes of poverty to create a more just and equitable world.

Key Statistics

  • Global poverty: About 9.2% of the world lives on less than $1.90 a day.
  • Child poverty: 356 million children live in extreme poverty globally.
  • U.S. poverty: Around 37.9 million people in the U.S. live below the poverty line.
